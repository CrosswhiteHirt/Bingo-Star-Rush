Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bingo Star Rush handles information. The game is designed as an offline, single-player Bingo experience.

Information We Collect

Bingo Star Rush does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, photos, or payment information.

Game Data

The game may save basic progress data locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, star results, and sound preferences. This data stays on your device and is used only to provide the game experience.

Network, Ads, and Purchases

  • The game does not require online login.
  • The game does not include in-app purchases.
  • The game does not use third-party advertising or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

Bingo Star Rush does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the game changes. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
