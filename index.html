About the Game

Bingo Star Rush is a single-player 75-ball Bingo game built for quick sessions and clear decisions. Watch the current call, mark matching numbers on your 5x5 card, and press Bingo when your line is ready.

Levels gradually increase the pace with faster calls, stricter mistake limits, and star-based results that reward accurate play.

Classic Bingo RulesMark B-I-N-G-O columns from 1 to 75 with a free center space.
Rush-Level ChallengeCalls speed up as you progress through tougher stages.
Offline PlayEnjoy the game anytime without accounts, purchases, or online competition.
